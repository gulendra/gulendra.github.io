Loksabha Election 2019

India is the world's largest democracy in the world and like any democracy, it depends on the electoral process to decide who will hold the power in the country. Elections for Parliament, Rajya Sabha, Lok Sabha, the Legislative Assemblies, and numerous other Councils and local bodies are such examples of the electoral process. Among all the elections Lok Sabha election is the most crucial because the results of Lok Sabha election decides who will become the Prime Minister Of India (PM). Lok Sabha is called the Lower House of the parliament and also known as the popular house because it represents the people of India as a whole.
